How We Became Bernard and Bianca

Bernard&Bianca stands for natural, sustainable, compassionate and reliable fragrances. We are committed to creating each product with love and care and delivering it to you, in order for you to go out in the world with the courage and passion to create meaningful relationships. 

Our vision says it all: “In a world where individuals date to not be lonely, we believe that loneliness can be overcome by pursuing meaningful and uplifting relationships because only then will one understand the value of companionship, and learn to love”. 

Inspired by the movie Bernard&Bianca from 1977, our message is to give every relationship the last push towards establishing a meaningful and uplifting bond. By choosing Bernard&Bianca, you choose to open yourself up to creating the most beautiful bonds with the people around you. 

The smell of love is an organic unisex perfume, out of amber wood, citrus, green pepper and grapefruit. The subtle blend of each produces a light and fresh fragrance.
